About Me

Hi! I'm James Louie Lacerna, affectionately known as Kenrich among my friends. Celebrating my birthday on June 10, 2005, I proudly identify as a lively Gemini, currently reveling in the vibrant age of 18. Nestled in the scenic locale of San Ignacio, Rosario, Batangas, I find solace and inspiration in this beautiful corner of the world. Embarking on the exciting journey of higher education, I am passionately pursuing my first year in Computer Science at Batangas State University - The National Engineering University, Alangilan Campus.
